Productivity Foundations

The technique. Simplified.

Nimbus is built around the original core loop of the Pomodoro technique—eliminating unnecessary features to help you enter flow faster.

01

Scope One Goal

Pick a single objective. Input the task description inside Nimbus to cement your commitment.

02

Work Unbroken

Press Play and execute for 25 minutes. If distraction strikes, let the expanding color dial pull you back.

03

Step Away Completely

When the alarm signals, disengage. Rest for 5 minutes. Walk, stretch, breathe. Do not check social media.

04

Analyze the Trail

Track metrics over 4 sessions. Export logs to CSV to review exact start-times and session completions.
